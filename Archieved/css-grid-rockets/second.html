Artemis 1

Artemis 1 will be the most powerful rocket to have been launched. It is composed of two main stages, the core stage and the interim cyrogenic propulsion stage. It also has an Orion stage adapter which will help the Orion spacecraft on its mission.

NASA DART Test

NASA's DART Test was a recently conducted mission to test if kinetic impact can influence an asteroids trajectory. This the first test of many more to come. The test included ramming the spacecraft into the asteroid at an amazingly high speed of 3.8 miles per second!
