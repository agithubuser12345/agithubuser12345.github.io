Rockets

Hello! I am Anish! One thing I really care about and have a great passion for is rocketry. A whole infinite universe lies ahead of us. Humankind is on a mission to explore this, and learning about rockets, rocket propulsion, and the mysteries of the universe makes this my something I am really passionate about. Rocketry is the field of science that deals with rocket propulsion and the exploration of space using rockets/man-made machines. A rocket is a propellant that carries payload. The simplest rockets date back to the 1200s in China when they were used for fireworks, armies, and celebrations. As we progressed, the idea of rockets has changed. On March, 26th, 1926, Robert Goddard, an American physicist, launched the first liquid-fueled rocket. He is known as the father of rocket propulsion.

Some of the things you will learn by reading this website are the basic parts of rockets, how rockets get into orbit and beyond, and other useful information.
