Quick Information


    Artemis 1: Uncrewed flight test.

    Artemis 1 launched on November 15th, 2022.


    Artemis 2: Crewed flyby of the Moon.

    Artemis 2 is projected to take place around 2024.


    Artemis 3: Crewed lunar mission (since Apollo 11, 1972).

    Artemis 3 is projected to take place around 2025.


    Artemis 4: Crewed mission to the Lunar Gateway Station.

    Artemis 4 is projected to take place around 2027.
