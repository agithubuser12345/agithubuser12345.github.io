Artemis Program

The main purpose of the Artemis Program is to send astronauts back to the moon. In 1961, the Apollo program took the first astronauts to land on Moon (Neil Armstrong and Edwin "Buzz" Aldrin). Whatever scientists plan to do on Mars, they must master it on the Moon. From anywhere to sending humans on long distance travel to Mars, to collecting data from Mar's orbit, Artemis is a stepping stone for advancements in space.

Throughout this website, users can find information about each of the planned Artemis missions, learn more from the resources and videos, and view a gallery of images giving them a better idea of what this program is all about. And if needed, feel free to contact us. Enjoy!
