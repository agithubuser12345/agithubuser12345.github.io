About Me

I'm from Washington state and have heritage and family from India. I have many different interests in STEM topics and occasionally dive into wherever my interests take me.

Currently, I attend Eastside Preparatory School in Kirkland, WA. I'm new to Eastside Preparatory School (EPS) but have met new people and made new friends. When I'm at school, I enjoy different classes like PreCalculus where we learn about sine and cosine waves along with more applications of trignometric identities and equations.

In other classes like history, we look into European colonialism and the history behind the era of conquest and colonialism with powers like Spain and Portugal and their notorious history with indigenous populations. Overall, the teachers at this school are helpful and so far school is going well.

Some of my favorite activities/hobbies outside of school are spending time with family. Whether it's taking a small road trip or walking on a nearby trail to taking an international trip across the seas, I enjoy being able to spend time with family during breaks or when I get the chance. Additionally, I like reading books of different generes and writing poetry on various topics.

One of my big interest is rocketry. Rocketry is an overlap between different fields like aerospace and physics applied to experiment with rockets and other science related to outer space. I also enjoy looking at other topics related to rockets like orbital mechanics, along with learning about other celestial objects in outer space. There is so much to learn about these topics and rocketry has become a big part of my interests. Recently, NASA has launched the Artemis program which looks to get humans back to the Moon and explore the lunar surface ("NASA Artemis"). Artemis 1 was launched a few months ago on November 15th, 2022. The mission was a success and the Orion spacecraft returned safely. NASA has many other interesting Artemis missions in the future including the Lunar Gateway Station in orbit around the Moon.

On a different topic, sports are one of my hobbies and favorite things to do with my family and friends.

The main sports I enjoy playing are cricket and basketball. I normally play cricket with my family and friends, but I practice basketball occasionally on my own. Watching sports like cricket is also fun, especially keeping track of matches and certain parts of cricket like IPL. India is currently finishing up a test match series against Australia as they lead 2-1.
